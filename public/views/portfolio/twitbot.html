twitter bots

  • I've built three Twitter bots that use the Twitter API and then post specific content. SpaceLoversBot (pictured on the tablet above) is the most popular bot. It's a Ruby built bot and retweets people saying "I love space." ITooLoveBot finds people saying "I love" and an adjective and a noun, then tweets, "I too love [adjective] [noun], user." GFoustBot uses the Wordnik API to riff on the poem "But the ghosts, they own everything" by Graham Foust. All three are hosted on Heroku and incorporate cron jobs to keep their feeds fresh.
  • Languages: JavaScript, Ruby
  • APIs: Twitter, Wordnik
